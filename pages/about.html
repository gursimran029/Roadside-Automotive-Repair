About Us

The Auto Shop is home to the best auto repair Canada has to offer. We're certain that you'll notice that we're not just another run of the mill repair shop the second you arrive. We know that great service starts with treating you right. We do that by offering fast, friendly service at very competitive pricing.
Our difference is our commitment to providing you value. We know how to extend the life of your car and keep your repair expenses to a minimum. Our professional auto technicians, our first line products, and our 3 year/60,000 km warranty on every service we provide are part of our commitment to extending you that value.
Our game changing service - we offer free 3rd party protection plan warranty coverage with our standard fluid services! This package of services is something that we feel makes a real difference in keeping your maintenance costs low. Our advantage is our free extended protection plan coverage of up to $20,000 per car - for the life of your car. We regularly provide brake system repair, steering system repair, and coolant system repair under full warranty for folks that have followed our basic maintenance plan. We've saved hundreds of our customers - thousands of dollars in repair cost.
We're about providing your with more than just great service - we are committed to providing you value with every service we do.

Our Hello

Our friendly front counter staff is completely focussed on customer service. Our first order of business is to get you back to your busy life with one of our free loaner cars or courtesy shuttle service. We will give you a car to drive or drop you off and pick you up from work, from home or anywhere in Halton. There is no need to wait and sit idle while your car is being serviced. We understand exactly how busy life can get and we keep you mobile.

Our Communication

We stay in constant contact with you as our technicians inspect your vehicle and prepare a full digital report complete with photographs and video. We send those pictures and video directly to you by text or email so you know what we know. We follow up with a written estimate on any necessary repairs an ensure you fully understand the condition of your vehicle.
